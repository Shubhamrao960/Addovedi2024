ABOUT ADDOVEDI

ABOUT US

With the aim of fostering innovation, creativity, and technological advancement, NIT Arunachal Pradesh proudly presents the annual technical fest—Addovedi. This event has rapidly become a beacon of ingenuity, drawing participation from students across the nation.
Featuring an exciting array of technical workshops, hackathons, and competitions, Addovedi challenges participants to think outside the box and collaborate in pursuit of solutions that can transform industries and communities.
Join us in this exhilarating journey at Addovedi, where the future of technology unfolds, and together, we strive to harness the power of innovation for a better tomorrow.

We, the Faculty Heads of Addovedi, on behalf of the entire team, are immensely proud to announce that the Annual Technical Fest of NIT Arunachal Pradesh— Addovedi—will be held on 1st - 2nd November. With each passing year, Addovedi has grown exponentially, becoming a beacon of technological innovation and creativity, and we are thrilled by the overwhelming response from participants across the nation. The enthusiasm and support for Addovedi in the previous editions motivate us to bring you an even grander version this year. The dedicated team behind Addovedi is working tirelessly to ensure that the event surpasses expectations, offering a diverse range of technical workshops, hackathons, and competitions. With exciting projects, groundbreaking innovations, and opportunities to collaborate, Addovedi promises to be a transformative experience for all participants.
